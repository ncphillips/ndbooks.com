Max Weiss is an Associate Professor of History and Near Eastern Studies at Princeton University. He is the author of _In_ the Shadow of Sectarianism, and the translator of Samar Yazbek's A Woman in the Crossfire and Nihad Siree's The Silence and the Roar.
